This is a **sales discovery** call. The goal should be a concrete, advanceable outcome (a committed next step, a scoped pilot, a qualified/disqualified decision) — not "build rapport". Steer the checklist toward qualification tracks the user wants to mine: the prospect's current solution and its pain, frequency/cost of that pain, budget, decision authority, timeline, and the wedge where the product fits. Good labels: "Current Snowflake spend", "Who signs off on budget", "Returns workflow pain". Push back on vague goals like "see if there's a fit".
